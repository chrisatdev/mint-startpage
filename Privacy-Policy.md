# Privacy Policy for Mint Startpage

**Last updated**: January 7, 2025

## Overview

Mint Startpage is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our browser extension handles your data.

## Data Collection

**We DO NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

Mint Startpage:

- ❌ Does NOT collect personal information
- ❌ Does NOT track your browsing activity
- ❌ Does NOT use cookies or analytics
- ❌ Does NOT send data to external servers
- ❌ Does NOT sell or share your data with third parties

## Data Storage

All data is stored **locally on your device** using Chrome's Storage API:

- **Groups and links**: Saved locally to remember your organization
- **Theme preferences**: Colors, gradients, or background images stored locally
- **Settings**: All configuration stored on your device only

**Your data never leaves your computer.**

## Permissions Used

The extension requests the following permission:

### Storage Permission

- **Purpose**: To save your groups, links, and theme preferences
- **Scope**: Local storage only (chrome.storage.local)
- **Data**: Group names, link titles/URLs, theme settings
- **Access**: Only you can access this data on your device

## Third-Party Services

### Favicons

The extension loads website favicons (small icons) using:

1. Google Favicon Service (`https://www.google.com/s2/favicons`)
2. Direct domain requests (`https://example.com/favicon.ico`)

These requests are made to display website icons for your links. No personal data is sent with these requests—only the domain name of websites you've added.

## Data Export and Deletion

You have full control over your data:

- **Export**: Download all your data as a JSON file at any time
- **Import**: Restore your data from a previous backup
- **Delete**: Use the "Reset all" option to delete all stored data
- **Uninstall**: Removing the extension deletes all local data automatically
